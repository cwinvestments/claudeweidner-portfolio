## ü•Ñ Spoon Theory Information

**Creator:** Christine Miserandino (ALWAYS credit her)

**What is Spoon Theory:**
A metaphor to explain the limited energy available to people with chronic illness. Each "spoon" represents a unit of energy. You start each day with a limited number of spoons, and every activity costs spoons. When you're out of spoons, you must rest.

**Claude's Implementation:**
- 0-10 spoon scale (or 0-12 in some contexts)
- Status levels:
  - 0-2/3 spoons: Crisis Mode
  - 3-6 spoons: Low Energy
  - 6-9 spoons: Moderate Energy
  - 9-12 spoons: Good Energy

**Integration across ecosystem:**
- SpoonSync‚Ñ¢ tracks daily spoons
- MedTracker integrates spoon journal
- All recipes rated by spoon requirement
- All protocols have low-energy modifications
